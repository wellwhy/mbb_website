Manon Macasaet is a New York-based multi-media artist and videographer best known for her collaborative work with Stussy, X-Girl, MadeMe and more. Her first photo book, Sab & Alex, was released along with her debut show, Dreamhouse in 2018. Big Bad Wolf, a modern fairytale short film, p%iered at Anthology Film Archives in 2019. As an artist, her work focuses on reflections of youth & friendship, and Manon describes herself as building an idealized dream world that the viewer can step into. Manon has also modeled for brands including American Eagle, Fuct, Gucci, Adidas, and more, and appeared in magazines including DIS, Dazed, i-D, Nylon Japan, Holiday, Document Journal, Wonderland, and many others.
